Description - StarBank is a hypothetical Bank where customers can open checking and/or savings accounts. When a customer opens a savings account, his account is also credited with interest. Star Bank has several emplooyees and ATMS that helps customers to maintain their checking and savings accounts. and each transaction is recorded through transaction ID. The Bank keeps track of all the customer and employee information including first name, last name, address and email address.

 

Use Cases:

As a customer, I want to open an account.

As a customer, I want to delete an account.

As a customer,  I want to withdraw money from an account through ATM machine.

As a customer,  I want to deposit money to an account through ATM machine.

As a customer, I want to check my balance through ATM machine.

As a customer,  I want to withdraw money from an account by visiting the Bank

As a customer,  I want to deposit money to an account by visiting the Bank

As a customer, I want to check my balance by visiting the Bank

As a customer, I want to keep track of all my transactions.

As a customer, I want to open either checkings or savings account, or both.

As an employee, I want to to add a customer (open an account).

As an employee, I want to delete a customer (delete an account).

As an employee, I want to open an account for the customer.

As an employee, I want to delete an account for the customer.

As an employee, I want to keep track of all the transactions for the customer.

As an employee, I want to get the total amount of transactions per day.

As an employee, I want to add an employee.

As an employee, I want to get rid of an employee.

As an employee, I want to add ATM machines.
